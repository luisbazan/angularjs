Quality Assurance Environments

Quality Assurance environments are used for system integration testing and user acceptance testing. The QA1/QA2 environments are generally used for load testing. QA3 and QA7 are masked data environments. QA4, QA5 and QA6 are general testing areas. These environments are on a three month rotation cycle. The database schemas follow each environment as well. Profiling clients are special version of that client that provides profiling (timing) information about ETMS transactions.
